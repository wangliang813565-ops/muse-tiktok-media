Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 14, 2026

MUSE Trendy Toy ("we", "our", or "us") operates social media accounts to share content about our designer toy products. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in connection with our social media publishing tools.

1. Information We Collect

Our social media publishing tools interact with platform APIs (TikTok, Instagram, Facebook) to publish content on our own brand accounts. We do not collect personal information from end users through these integrations.

The only data we access through platform APIs includes:

  • Our own account authentication credentials (access tokens)
  • Our own published content metadata (post IDs, timestamps, engagement metrics)
  • Public comments on our own posts (usernames and comment text)

2. How We Use Information

We use platform APIs solely to:

  • Publish photos, videos, and text content to our brand social media accounts
  • Monitor engagement metrics on our published content
  • Read and respond to comments on our own posts

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share any user data with third parties. Our API integrations are used exclusively for managing our own brand social media presence.

4. Data Security

We store API credentials securely using encrypted storage with restricted file permissions. Access tokens are refreshed automatically and stored locally on our secure systems.

5. Third-Party Platforms

When you interact with our content on social media platforms, your interaction is governed by that platform's own privacy policy:

  • TikTok Privacy Policy
  • Instagram Privacy Policy
  • Facebook Privacy Policy

6. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
